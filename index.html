Privacy Policy for Nwbty

Effective Date: January 10, 2026

1. Introduction

We value your trust in using the Nwbty app. This policy is designed to explain how we handle data, emphasizing our full commitment to protecting your privacy and health information.

2. Information Collection and Use

Nwbty is a local logging tool. We do not collect any Personally Identifiable Information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number. All data you enter (such as seizure logs, descriptions, and medication doses) is stored exclusively locally on your personal device.

3. Data Storage and Security

Since all data is stored entirely within your device's memory, we (as developers) have no access to view or retrieve these logs. You are primarily responsible for the security of your device. We do not upload your data to any external servers, nor do we share it with any third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses the Flutter framework and essential core libraries required for operation. It does not use tracking tools, analytics, or advertisements that collect your personal data.

5. User Rights

You have full control over your data; you can edit or delete it manually at any time within the app. Please note that if you uninstall the app from your device, all locally stored data will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.
